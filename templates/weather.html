Quick Tips for Astronomy Weather Planning

  • Check Clear Outside for astronomy-specific conditions (cloud cover, seeing, transparency)
  • Monitor wind speeds - high winds can affect telescope stability
  • Watch for jet stream patterns that can impact seeing conditions
  • Compare multiple sources for the most reliable forecast
  • Satellite imagery helps identify actual cloud movements
